About

Glyphy is a simple online tool that allows you to easily copy special characters & symbols (also known as glyphs) to your device's clipboard so that you can then paste them to any document or app you want. It doesn't contain every possible glyph out there (yet!) ‚Äì and we have purposely omitted some that you can easily access from your keyboard ‚Äì however if you would like to see others added to the list let us know‚Üí‚Äç
